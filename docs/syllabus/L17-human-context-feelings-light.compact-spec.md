## 2. Owned Target

**Social check-in chunks (primary) + one capped positive adjective + recycled states (no new system):**

- **Social check-in (new, the freshness hook):** `Ça va ?` (asking) · `Ça va.` (I'm okay) · `Ça ne va pas.` (I'm not okay) — **frozen chunks**; `ça va` is a set social phrase, **not** productive `aller` and **not** futur proche.
- **Positive feeling (new — one adjective, gendered alternants):** `Je suis content.` (m) / `Je suis contente.` (f) in the **owned `je suis + state` frame** — the learner's first *positive* feeling. **The gender agreement is shown as a fixed pair, NOT a taught rule** — same discipline as `fatigué` (owned masculine-default since L2); the adjective-agreement *system* stays deferred.
- **Recycled / recombined states (no re-teaching):** `je suis fatigué` (L2/L6) · `je ne suis pas prêt` (L6) · `j'ai besoin d'aide` (L4/L6) · `j'ai faim` / `j'ai peur` (L4) · `je ne comprends pas` (L1) · `c'est pas grave` (L2/L6) · `il faut faire une pause` (L15, obligation) · `est-ce que vous pouvez répéter ?` (L11/L12) · `j'y vais` / `on y va` (L14, only if the place is already clear).
- **Light promotion:** `je comprends` (affirmative) — currently frozen inside `je ne comprends pas`; surfaces lightly. **Does not open a `comprendre` paradigm.**

**Clarify:**
- L17 owns **simple human-state response**, **not emotional depth**.
- The learner should feel: **"I can check in and respond humanly — without giving advice."**
- The only genuinely new lexis is **`ça va` (chunk-set)** + **`content`/`contente` (one adjective)**; everything else is recombination.

## 3. Not Owned / Deferred (explicit)

Recognition-only or deferred — never production targets in L17:
- **broad emotion vocabulary** — `triste`/`anxieux`/`déprimé`/`seul`/`énervé`/`déçu` as an active set; **`triste` is recognition only**; no word-list.
- **`se sentir` / `je me sens …`** — reflexive-verb system; **recognition / future-hook only**, never produced.
- **advice / conditionnel** (`tu devrais` / `il faudrait` / `je devrais`) — the band map's "light advice", **deferred to paid-zone** (L15).
- **therapy / coaching / moralizing tone** (incl. moralizing via `il faut`/`je dois`).
- **emotional personalization** (tailoring feelings content to the learner).
- **past/future feeling narrative** (`hier j'étais triste`, `demain ça ira`, `ça va aller`) — feelings stay **present**.
- **futur proche** (`je vais + inf.`) — **recognition / future-hook for L18 only**, never owned here.
- **`je vais bien`** (the `aller`-state answer to `ça va ?`) — **recognition/deferred** (reuses `aller` in a non-movement sense + adds `bien`); owned answers are `ça va` / `je suis content(e)`.
- **adjective gender-agreement system** — `content`/`contente` shown as a fixed pair; the agreement *rule* is not taught.
- **object-pronoun production** (`je peux vous aider` / `aidez-moi`) — `m'aider` stays frozen inside `vous pouvez m'aider`.
- **full / deep A Small Moment emotional recurrence** + **paid-depth AI emotional/social feedback** (paid-zone).

## 5. Candidate Scene / Sentence Family

> Scene (small human moment, after class / before leaving): *"You check in with someone. They're not okay — tired, don't understand. You reassure them; one must take a break; then you head off."* The check-in (`Ça va ?` → feeling answer) is the spine.

| Role | Sentence | Note |
|---|---|---|
| **Check-in (anchor, new)** | `Ça va ?` | **new core**: social check-in, frozen chunk |
| **Negative answer (recycled state)** | `Ça ne va pas. Je suis fatigué.` | `ça ne va pas` + L2/L6 `fatigué` |
| **Positive answer (new adjective)** | `Ça va. Je suis content.` *(/ `contente`)* | `ça va` + new `content(e)` |
| **Repair (recycled)** | `Je ne comprends pas. Vous pouvez répéter ?` | L1 / L11–L12 |
| **Reassure (recycled)** | `C'est pas grave.` | L2/L6 |
| **Obligation, NOT advice (recycled)** | `Il faut faire une pause.` | L15 — "one must," **not** "you should" |
| **Leave (recycled, optional)** | `J'y vais.` | L14 place-`y` — only if the place is already clear |

- **Anchor:** `Ça va ?` · **Variations:** `Ça va.`, `Ça ne va pas.`, `Je suis content(e).`, `Je suis fatigué.` · **Contrasts:** `Ça va ?` (frozen social) vs `Je vais à la maison.` (movement `aller`, L7 — *different `aller`*); `Il faut faire une pause.` (obligation) vs `Tu devrais faire une pause.` (**advice — forbidden**).
- **Interchangeable pieces:** `je suis { content(e) / fatigué / (neg) prêt }` · `ça va` / `ça ne va pas` · (recycled tail) `je ne comprends pas` / `c'est pas grave` / `j'y vais`.
- **Avoid / forbidden:** `Je me sens triste.` (se sentir) · `Tu devrais faire une pause.` (advice) · `Hier, j'étais triste.` / `Demain, ça ira.` / `Ça va aller.` (past/future) · `Je suis anxieux / déprimé / seul …` (emotion dump) · any emotional-advice / therapy-style reply · `Je peux vous aider.` (object pronoun) · `Je vais bien.` (aller-state, recognition).

## 9. Natural Reveal / Feedback

**Check-in / feeling (signature):**
- **Expected:** `Ça va ?` / `Ça va.` / `Ça ne va pas. Je suis fatigué.` / `Je suis content(e).`
- **Acceptable alternatives:** `Non, ça ne va pas`; the owned states (`je suis fatigué` / `j'ai besoin d'aide`) as answers; `ça va bien` *(light — `bien` recognition)*.

**Common mistakes (watch list):**
- **over-answering with new emotions** (importing `anxieux`, `déprimé`, `seul`…) — hint: "say it with what you know — `je suis content(e)` / `je suis fatigué`." (`trap:emotion-vocab-dump`)
- **using `se sentir` too early** (`Je me sens fatigué.`) — hint: "for now, `je suis fatigué`." (`trap:se-sentir-too-early`)
- **giving advice with `tu devrais`** (`Tu devrais faire une pause.`) — hint: "say how you feel, or what *one must* do (`il faut faire une pause`) — not advice." (`trap:conditionnel-advice-too-early`)
- **using past/future** (`Hier, j'étais triste.` / `Demain, ça ira.`) — hint: "stay in the present — `ça va` / `ça ne va pas`." (`trap:past-future-feeling-narrative`)
- **turning `ça va` into `aller`/futur logic** (`Ça va aller.` / `Je vais bien` as a taught form) — hint: "`ça va` is a set phrase; keep it as is." (`trap:ca-va-aller-future-leak`)
- **object pronoun** (`Je peux vous aider.`) — hint: "keep `m'aider` inside `vous pouvez m'aider`; offer help with `je peux faire ça`." (`trap:object-pronoun-pressure-too-early`)
- **therapy/coach tone / writing too long** — one short, kind line; passive mirror only; no coaching.

> Feedback style: **passive mirror**; "our expected target was…"; **natural alternative if within scope**; recognition forms (`je me sens`, `tu devrais`, `ça va aller`, `je vais bien`) are **redirected, never taught on the spot** (contract §11). **No score / reward / streak language; no therapy/coach tone.**

## 12. QA / Pilot Findings

- **Is a compact spec enough?** **Yes.** 0 new systems, 0 new architecture verbs, ≤2 new lexical items over owned frames; mostly recombination. A full spec would invite the emotion-vocabulary tables the gate forbids.
- **Is L17 too light after L16?** **No — appropriately light, and differentiated.** L16 was integration + reading seed; L17 adds a genuinely *new human capability* (the social check-in `ça va ?`) plus the first *positive* feeling — neither of which L16 covered. It is recycle-dominant but not flat: the learner can now open and answer "how are you?", which the free tier could not do before.
- **Does `ça va` risk confusing `aller`/futur?** **A real but contained risk.** `ça va` literally is `aller` 3rd-person, and `ça va aller` (futur proche) is the natural drift. Contained by: owning `ça va` as a **frozen chunk** (homograph guard, distinct from `word:aller`), blocking `ça va aller` / `je vais bien` (`trap:ca-va-aller-future-leak`), and keeping `aller` movement-only (L7). Worth watching in smoke; if confusion appears, lean harder on "set phrase, learn it whole."
- **Does `content`/`contente` add enough novelty without becoming a vocab dump?** **Yes — it is the right amount.** One positive adjective gives the learner their first *positive* self-state (all prior states are negative/neutral) — high emotional usefulness, near-zero load. The gendered pair is shown as a fixed alternant, not an agreement rule. More adjectives would tip into a dump; `triste` stays recognition-only.
- **Is advice fully blocked?** **Yes** — conditionnel/advice deferred (paid-zone); `il faut`/`je dois` used as obligation, not coaching; `trap:conditionnel-advice-too-early`; passive mirror throughout.
- **Is `se sentir` fully blocked?** **Yes** — recognition / future-hook only, 0 production; `trap:se-sentir-too-early`.
- **Should L18 remain Futur-proche stronger preview?** **Yes** — L17's `je vais + inf.` recognition hook sets it up; L17 does **not** own futur proche.
- **Top risks:** (1) **emotion-vocabulary dump** (the #1 leak — capped at `content`/`contente`); (2) **advice/therapy tone** (deferred); (3) **`ça va` → `aller`/futur confusion** (frozen-chunk guard + trap); (4) **`se sentir`** (recognition only); (5) **past/future feeling narrative + object-pronoun pressure**. All guarded by §3/§6/§9/§10 + six traps.
