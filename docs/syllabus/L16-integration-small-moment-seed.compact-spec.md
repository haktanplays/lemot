## 2. Owned Target

**Primary — recombination (no new system):** the skill is *chaining* owned engines into one after-class human moment, plus the recycled meta `phen:integration-review`. Recombined engines:
- **`je peux` / `je ne peux pas` / `vous pouvez m'aider`** (L11 — help/permission; `m'aider` frozen).
- **`est-ce que + [owned clause]`** (L12 — yes/no wrapper).
- **`j'y vais` / `on y va`** (L14 — place-`y`, antecedent owned).
- **`il faut + inf.` (primary) / `je dois + inf.` (supported)** (L15 — obligation-light).
- **repair / politeness** (L1/L11): `je ne comprends pas`, `vous pouvez répéter ?`, `c'est pas grave`, `bonjour`, `merci`.

**A Small Moment seed (the one bounded new beat):**
- **one ≤2–3 line reading** — present-only, known-items-only;
- **one short scaffolded learner response** — closed comprehension choice and/or one short line from owned frames;
- **deterministic / model-answer Natural Reveal** — a hardcoded model answer + bounded accepted alternatives; **no live AI**.

**Clarify:**
- **L16 owns the A Small Moment *ritual seed shape*, not the full feature.** The deep, AI-driven, personalized A Small Moment is **paid-zone** (band map §5); L16 ships only the small reading-and-respond *format*.
- The learner should feel: **"I can understand a small human situation and respond simply."** Not "I'm chatting with a bot," not "I'm on a mission," not "I'm being graded."
- The only genuinely new items are **meta** (`phen:a-small-moment-seed`, `phen:model-answer-only-reveal`); infinitives, frames, and lexis are all owned.

- **`il faut que` + subjunctive** (L15's #1 leak — no `que`-clause).
- **conditionnel / advice** (`je devrais` / `il faudrait` / `tu devrais`).
- **broad A Small Moment feature** (multi-passage, interactive, adaptive).
- **paid-depth Practice Pool / Challenge response intelligence** (open-ended AI-graded production).

## 5. Candidate Scene / Sentence Family

> Scene (after-class human context, continuous with L10/L13/L15): *"After class. Someone is tired. They take a break, then head home."* The A Small Moment read **is** the scene; the spine is **read → understand → respond simply**.

### 5.1 Micro-reading candidate evaluation (choose the safer)

| Candidate | Verdict | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **A** — `Je suis fatigué.` / `Il faut aller à la maison.` / `Est-ce que vous pouvez répéter ?` | ⚠️ owned but **contextually weak** | all three lines are owned + present-only, but `Est-ce que vous pouvez répéter ?` is incoherent in a reading (nothing has been said to repeat); it reads as a drill, not a moment |
| **B (as briefed)** — `Je suis fatigué.` / `Il faut faire une pause.` / `Après, je vais à la maison.` | 🚫 **leaks new vocab** | **`Après` (afterwards) is NOT owned in L1–L15** — a discourse adverb dump (`trap:new-vocab-dump`). Demote. |
| **B′ (CHOSEN — B with `Après` removed)** — `Je suis fatigué.` / `Il faut faire une pause.` / `Je vais à la maison.` | ✅ **chosen** | 3 lines, **present-only, 100% owned** (L2/L6 + L15 + L7), coherent human moment ("I'm tired. One must take a break. I'm going home.") |

**Chosen reading (B′):**
- `Je suis fatigué.` *(L2/L6 — state)*
- `Il faut faire une pause.` *(L15 — primary obligation, invariable)*
- `Je vais à la maison.` *(L7 — movement, present)*

> **Product-critical demotion (the point of evaluating):** the briefed variant's `Après,` is a **new-vocabulary leak** — exactly the A Small Moment risk (a "richer" reading sneaking in a connector/adverb). Removing it keeps the read strictly known-items-only. **If a connector is ever wanted, it needs its own lesson; not here.**

### 5.2 Learner response candidates (one short, scaffolded)

| Candidate response | Verdict | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| `Il faut faire une pause.` | ✅ in scope | L15 primary |
| `Je dois faire une pause.` / `Je dois aller à la maison.` | ✅ in scope | L15 supported (`je dois + owned inf.`) |
| `J'y vais.` | ✅ in scope **if** the place is already named | L14 place-`y`; keep antecedent clear |
| `Je ne comprends pas. Vous pouvez répéter ?` | ✅ in scope | L1/L11 repair (frozen) |
| **`Je peux vous aider.`** | 🚫 **avoid** | object-pronoun pressure (`vous aider` outside frozen `vous pouvez m'aider`) — offer help via `Je peux faire ça` instead |
| **`Je peux y aller.` / `Je dois y aller.`** | 🚫 **avoid** | `y`+pouvoir / `y`+devoir chains deferred (recognition only) |
| **`Il faut que tu partes.`** | 🚫 **avoid** | subjunctive / deeper clause deferred |
| **`Tu devrais…`** / `Hier…` / `Demain…` / `Plus tard…` | 🚫 **avoid** | advice (conditionnel) + past/future narrative drift |

- **Anchor (chained, recycled):** `Je suis fatigué, il faut faire une pause.`
- **A Small Moment response (signature):** model answer `Je dois faire une pause.` *(or `J'y vais.` if the place is clear)* — bounded accepted alternatives: `Il faut faire une pause.`, `Je vais à la maison.`
- **Comprehension check (closed):** "What does this person need to do?" → choose **`faire une pause`** among owned distractors (`faire ça` / `aller à la maison`).

## 9. Natural Reveal / Feedback

**A Small Moment response (signature):**
- **Expected:** `Je dois faire une pause.` *(or `J'y vais.` if the place is clear)*
- **Acceptable alternatives:** `Il faut faire une pause.`, `Je vais à la maison.` — all owned, present-only.

**Common mistakes (watch list):**
- **adding past/future to the response** (`Hier, j'ai fait une pause.` / `Je vais faire une pause` as future) — hint: "stay in the present — `il faut faire une pause`." (`trap:past-future-reading-leak`)
- **over-answering with new vocabulary** (importing feelings/topic words) — hint: "answer with what you already know." (`trap:new-vocab-dump`)
- **turning obligation into advice** (`Tu devrais faire une pause.`) — hint: "this is what you *have to* do, not advice." (`trap:conditionnel-advice-too-early`)
- **`y` with a modal** (`je dois y aller` / `je peux y aller`) — hint: "for now, `je dois aller à la maison` / `j'y vais`." (`trap:y-modal-chain-too-early`)
- **producing an object pronoun** (`Je peux vous aider.` / `il faut m'aider`) — hint: "keep `m'aider` only inside `vous pouvez m'aider`; offer help with `je peux faire ça`." (`trap:object-pronoun-pressure-too-early`)
- **treating A Small Moment as chat** (writing a multi-line conversation) — hint: "one short answer is enough." (`trap:generic-ai-chat-drift`)
- **moralizing with `il faut` / `je dois`** ("you must rest, it's important…") — passive mirror only; no coaching.
- **writing too long** — one short line; this is a small moment, not an essay.

> Feedback style: **passive mirror**; "our expected target was…"; **meaning-first but within allowed forms**; recognition forms (`il faut que`, `je devrais`, `je dois y aller`, free chat) are **redirected, never taught on the spot** (contract §11). **No scores / reward / streak language.**

## 12. QA / Pilot Findings

- **Is a compact spec enough?** **Yes.** 0 new systems, 0 new architecture verbs, ≈0 new lexis; one bounded meta beat over owned material. The compact form (integration weighting, light tables) fits; a full treatment would pad the seed into the very feature the gate forbids. Promote to full only if smoke shows the reading-response beat needs more scaffolding.
- **Is A Small Moment too early?** **No — as a `model-answer-only` seed.** It is **not** new (L6–L10 already carry the optional present-only reading micro-paragraph); L16 only **owns one such beat in-lesson**. The early-risk forms (free response, AI eval, personalization) are all deferred. *If the seed could not be `model-answer-only`, it would be too early — but it can.*
- **Does `model-answer-only` solve the pre-Campfire AI/paywall risk?** **Yes.** With no live AI at L16, there is **no chatbot surface to drift into** and **nothing paid-zone is spent** — the deterministic reveal gives the *form* while reserving the *intelligence* (live eval, personalization, free conversation) for the paid tier.
- **Does L16 preserve paid-depth value?** **Yes.** The deep, AI-driven, personalized A Small Moment (and open-ended Challenge response intelligence) stays paid-zone (band map §5). L16 spends only the small reading-and-respond slice.
- **Is object-pronoun pressure controlled?** **Yes.** `m'aider` stays frozen inside `vous pouvez m'aider`; `Je peux vous aider.` is demoted/blocked; help is offered via `Je peux faire ça` / `Il faut faire une pause` (`trap:object-pronoun-pressure-too-early`).
- **Is the reading present-only and known-items-only?** **Yes** — the briefed `Après,` variant was **demoted as a new-vocab leak**; the chosen read (`Je suis fatigué. / Il faut faire une pause. / Je vais à la maison.`) is 100% owned, present-only.
- **Should L17 remain Human Context / Feelings?** **Yes** — L16's forward hook lands on feelings (carried by owned `fatigué`, no new lexis); L17 then owns feelings-in-frames. L16 does not pre-empt it (no emotional-vocab dump here).
- **Top risks:** (1) **A Small Moment scope-creep into free-text / generic AI chat** (the #1 leak — guarded by `model-answer-only` + `trap:generic-ai-chat-drift`/`trap:mini-mission-resurrection`); (2) **past/future tense in the reading** (`trap:past-future-reading-leak`); (3) **new-vocab leak in the read** (the demoted `Après,`); (4) **object-pronoun pressure** (`vous aider`); (5) **obligation→advice slide** (`tu devrais`). All guarded by §3/§6/§9/§10 + seven traps.
