# L16 — Integration + A Small Moment Seed Gate Review / Pre-Spec Scope

> Follows `docs/learning-engine-v1.md` + `docs/syllabus/lesson-spec-template-v1.1.md` + `docs/syllabus/lesson-archetype-templates-v1.md` + `docs/syllabus/canonical-item-id-convention-v0.1.md` + `docs/syllabus/ai-generation-contract-v1.md` + `docs/syllabus/L10-L20-band-map-v0.md` + the L10 (after-class integration) / L13 (can-do + asking integration) / L14 (`y`-light) / L15 (devoir/falloir-light) specs.
> **Pre-spec planning/review only.** This is a **gate review**, NOT the L16 lesson spec. It authorizes **no** code, content, flag, or runtime change, and does **not** create `docs/syllabus/L16-*.lesson-spec.md` / `*.compact-spec.md`. Locked product canon wins on conflict. Dev APK runtime (L1–L5 only) is unaffected.

> **Why this gate exists.** The band map (Option C) pencils **L16 = "Integration + A Small Moment seed"** — the **rhythm beat** after the L14 `y` + L15 obligation run, and the **first time the signature reading-response ritual ("A Small Moment") becomes an owned in-lesson beat** rather than an optional Daily-Review micro-paragraph. A "new ritual" inside an integration lesson carries three product risks the band map waves at but does not resolve: **(1) feature creep** (A Small Moment becoming a full feature/launch), **(2) generic AI-chat drift** (a "respond to a small situation" beat sliding into open conversation), and **(3) paywall leakage** (giving away the deep, AI-driven reading-response experience the paid zone is supposed to own). This review decides **whether L16 should seed A Small Moment at all**, and if so, **how narrow the seed must be.**

## 1. Executive Summary

- **Should L16 be Integration + A Small Moment seed?** **Yes — conditionally.** Integration is correct and non-negotiable (L14 `y` + L15 devoir = 2 consecutive new-engine lessons → the Integration Rhythm Rule mandates a beat). The A Small Moment **seed is the right novelty device** for that beat **only because the seed can be made `model-answer-only` (no live AI)** — which structurally removes the AI-chat risk at L16. If the seed *required* live AI to work, this review would recommend pure integration (Option A) and defer A Small Moment to the paid zone. It does not require live AI, so **Option B (Integration primary + a strictly-bounded A Small Moment seed) is the recommendation.**
- **What exactly should L16 own?** (a) the **recombination skill** across L11–L15 (recycled `phen:integration-review`) in one after-class human scene; (b) **one** owned **A Small Moment beat** = a **≤2–3 line present-only, known-items-only reading micro-text** + **one short, scaffolded (closed or model-answer-only) learner response** — i.e. it **promotes the L6→L10 optional reading micro-paragraph into a first-class owned beat.** **0 new grammar systems, 0 new architecture verbs.**
- **What must remain deferred?** **Live AI evaluation / free-text response / personalization** (paid-zone, and no AI runs pre-L20 anyway); **open chat / free conversation**; **Mini Mission / Mini Conversation revival** (legacy, de-prioritized); **advice / moral / coaching tone** (the `il faut`/`je dois` trap); **new emotional vocabulary** (that is L17's job); **past/future in the reading text**; **`il faut que` + subjunctive**; **`y`+pouvoir/devoir chains**; **object-pronoun production** (`m'aider`/`vous aider` stay frozen/recognition). New-feature ownership = **light seed only**.
- **Is L16 safe after L14 `y` + L15 obligation-light?** **Yes**, on conditions: it owns **no** new system, the reading is **present-only / known-items-only**, the response is **scaffolded not free**, and it carries a **forward preview hook** (likely L17 feelings or L18 futur) to dodge the "too little novelty" risk — because **L13 was already a pure integration beat only three lessons earlier**, a *second* near-identical pure-integration L16 would feel repetitive. The A Small Moment seed is precisely what differentiates L16 from L13 **without** adding grammar.

> **Headline recommendation (product-critical, not flattering the band map).** The band map's "Integration + A Small Moment seed" is right, but its phrasing **understates the danger and overstates the feature.** Two sharpenings: **(1)** the seed must be defined as a **reading-comprehension-led, model-answer-only** beat — *not* "AI/Natural Reveal may reflect meaning" (that implies AI evaluation, which is paid-zone). At L16 there is **no AI**; "reflect meaning" must mean a **deterministic model-answer reveal**, full stop. **(2)** A Small Moment is **not new** — L6, L7, L8, L9, L10 already carry an *optional* present-only reading micro-paragraph in Daily Review. L16 should **own that existing shape**, not invent a feature. Framed this way, the seed is low-risk; framed as "introduce the signature reading-response ritual," it invites exactly the creep/chat/leak this gate exists to stop.

## 3. Candidate L16 Options

### Option A — Pure Integration only (no A Small Moment)
**Own:** recombine L14 `y` + L15 obligation (with L11–L13) in an after-class scene; no reading-response beat.
- **Strengths:** maximally safe; zero feature/chat/leak risk; cleanest scope; identical to the proven L10/L13 shape.
- **Weaknesses:** **near-duplicate of L13** (a pure integration beat only 3 lessons earlier) → the archetype's named **"too little novelty"** risk compounds; **dangles A Small Moment**, which **L19 is slated to *recur*** — if L16 never seeds it, L19 has nothing to recur and the band loses its one non-grammar ritual; under-delivers the band's signature differentiator.
- **Verdict:** **the safety fallback** — adopt **only if** the seed cannot be kept `model-answer-only`/closed. Otherwise it wastes the slot.

### Option B — Integration + A Small Moment seed (RECOMMENDED)
**Own:** integration as **primary** (the recombination skill, archetype #10 weighting) + **one** very short A Small Moment beat as a **seed**: a **≤2–3 line present-only, known-items-only reading micro-text** + **one short scaffolded (closed/model-answer-only) response**.
- **Is it too much?** **Only if the seed is mis-scoped.** Kept to one reading beat with **0 new systems** and **model-answer-only** feedback, the genuinely-new load is **one meta-phenomenon** (`phen:a-small-moment-seed`) + a reading `sent:` — comparable to L10's "2 meta, 0 lexis." It is **not** a new feature launch; it is **promoting an existing optional reading into an owned beat.**
- **Strengths:** differentiates L16 from L13 **without** adding grammar (archetype #10's preferred novelty device); lands the L6-seeded reading; sets up the L19 recurrence; gives the free band a distinctive ritual no grammar lesson provides.
- **Risks:** feature creep, chat drift, paywall leakage, past-tense-in-reading — **all guarded** (§5/§7/§8/§9) and **structurally limited by the no-AI-pre-paywall fact.**
- **Verdict:** **adopt (strictly bounded seed).**

### Option C — A Small Moment as a bigger feature lesson
**Own:** A Small Moment as a substantial reading + interactive-response feature (multiple passages, learner free response, meaning reflection).
- **Too early, and too AI-chat-like.** A "bigger" A Small Moment needs **AI evaluation** to grade free responses and "reflect meaning" — but **L0–L20 run `model-answer-only` (no live AI)** and **live AI evaluation is paid-zone** (band map §5; AI contract §3 `feedback_mode`). A bigger feature at L16 would therefore either (a) require shipping paid-zone AI early (leak) or (b) fake "reflection" with a chatty deterministic surface that drifts toward the banned chatbot register. It also violates "light seed."
- **Verdict:** **reject.** This is exactly the creep the gate prevents. The "big" A Small Moment is a **paid-zone** experience.

### Option D — Move A Small Moment later (L19 or post-Campfire)
**Own:** L16 = pure integration; first A Small Moment appears at L19 (or post-Campfire).
- **Evaluation:** the deep/AI A Small Moment **is already reserved for the paid zone regardless** (band map §5), so deferring the **seed** protects nothing extra — it only **costs** the free band its novelty and **breaks the L19 recurrence** (L19 = "Integration / weak-point repair / A Small Moment" *recurs* a thing L16 was meant to seed). Deferring the seed to L19 just renames Option A's problem and pushes it one beat.
- **Verdict:** **reject** as a *seed-delay*; the paid-zone deferral of the *deep* version is already correct and unchanged.

**Recommendation: Option B** — Integration primary + a strictly-bounded, `model-answer-only` A Small Moment seed; **Option A as the fallback** if review decides even the bounded seed is premature.

## 5. What A Small Moment Means Here (strict seed definition)

A Small Moment at L16 is **one owned beat**, defined tightly:

- a **short reading or micro-situation**: **≤2–3 lines**, **present-tense only**, built from **already-owned L1–L16 material** (it reads like the L6–L10 reading micro-paragraph, now owned);
- the learner gives **one short response** — either **chooses what they understood** (closed comprehension) or **says/writes one short line** using **owned** frames;
- **feedback is `model-answer-only`** — a **deterministic** reveal of meaning + a model response + bounded accepted alternatives (Natural Reveal contract §10). **"Reflect meaning" must NOT mean live AI evaluation.**
- it should **feel like "respond to a small human situation,"** **not** a mission, **not** a chat, **not** a game.

**Classification of A Small Moment capabilities for the *seed*:**

| Capability | Classification (seed) | Note |
|---|---|---|
| **Reading comprehension** | **ACTIVE** | the core of the seed — comprehension-led, present-only, known-items-only |
| **Short learner response** | **SUPPORTED** | scaffolded — closed choice or short owned-frame line + model answer; **not** free production |
| **AI feedback** | **DEFERRED** | no live AI pre-paywall; L16 is `model-answer-only` (AI contract §3) |
| **Advice-like response** | **DEFERRED** | banned tone — the `il faut`/`je dois` moralizing trap (L15) |
| **Open chat** | **DEFERRED** | not a conversation surface |
| **Free conversation** | **DEFERRED** | paid-zone + identity violation |
| **Personalization** | **DEFERRED** | paid-zone (deeper AI-driven A Small Moment) |
| **Weak-point tagging** | **RECOGNITION / DEFERRED** | the beat may feed the *existing* Daily-Review/weak-point mechanism passively (like any lesson); **no** A Small Moment-specific adaptive tagging/intelligence in the seed |

## 6. Candidate L16 Scene (evaluation of the brief's example)

**Scene (in scope):** *"After class, someone is tired. They need to go home, but first they need help."* — pure after-class human context, continuous with L10/L13/L15.

**Reading micro-text (the A Small Moment read) — evaluated:**

| Line | Verdict | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| `Je suis fatigué.` | ✅ in scope | L2/L6 state (recycled) |
| `Il faut aller à la maison.` | ✅ in scope | L15 `il faut + aller` (present, invariable) |
| `Est-ce que vous pouvez m'aider ?` | ✅ in scope | L12 `est-ce que` + L11 frozen `vous pouvez m'aider` (owned in L13) |

**Learner response possibilities — evaluated (demotions are the point):**

| Candidate response | Verdict | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| `Il faut faire une pause.` | ✅ in scope | L15 primary (`il faut + faire une pause`) |
| `Je ne comprends pas. Vous pouvez répéter ?` | ✅ in scope | L1/L11 repair (frozen) |
| `J'y vais.` | ✅ in scope **if** the place is already named | L14 place-`y`; keep the antecedent clear (don't strand `y`) |
| **`Je peux vous aider.`** | 🚫 **DEMOTE — out of scope** | **`vous aider` stresses the object pronoun outside its frozen chunk.** L15's spec *deliberately avoided* `Il faut m'aider` for exactly this reason; `vous aider` is worse (different object pronoun, productive). There is **no owned "I can help you"** because object pronouns are not owned. Use `Je peux faire ça.` / `Il faut faire une pause.` instead. |

**Within-scope verdict:** the scene works almost entirely from owned material. **The single demotion** (`Je peux vous aider`) is instructive — it shows the **object-pronoun-pressure** leak A Small Moment invites: a "help" situation tempts `m'aider`/`vous aider`/`t'aider` out of their frozen chunk. **Keep `m'aider` frozen inside `vous pouvez m'aider` only; offer help via `Je peux faire ça` / `Il faut faire une pause`, never via a productive object pronoun.** Anything leaking object pronouns, broad `y`, advice register, or new vocab is **demoted to recognition or dropped.**

## 8. Paywall / Commercial Depth Check

- **Does seeding A Small Moment before Campfire ~L24 give away too much?** **No** — *because the seed is the cheap part.* The seed is a **`model-answer-only`, present-only, known-items-only reading + scaffolded response**. The **valuable** A Small Moment — **live AI evaluation, free-response handling, meaning reflection, personalization, adaptive difficulty, weak-point-driven reading selection** — stays **paid-zone** (band map §5: *"deeper AI-driven A Small Moment → paid-zone deepened"*; AI Natural Reveal depth: *model-answer-only L0–L20; live AI evaluation paid*). You give away the **form**, not the **intelligence.**
- **Does withholding A Small Moment make the free path less distinctive?** **Yes, slightly** — it is the band's **one non-grammar ritual**, and without it L16 is a near-duplicate of L13's pure integration. Seeding the form gives the free tier a recognizable, calming reading ritual that **builds desire for the AI-driven paid version** (the same preview-builds-desire logic the band map applies to the L18 futur preview).
- **What is the safest commercial compromise?** **Seed the *shape* (scripted reading + closed/short `model-answer-only` response), reserve the *AI* (evaluation, personalization, free conversation, adaptive reading).** This mirrors how `pouvoir`/`devoir`/`y` were each seeded as a light slice with the deep system reserved.
- **Should deeper AI personalization remain paid-zone?** **Yes — unambiguously.** It is the natural paid surface (live `ai-evaluation` `feedback_mode`), consistent with band map §5 and the AI contract. The seed must not pre-spend it.

## 11. Detailed Spec Recommendation

**Recommendation: write a COMPACT L16 spec next** — Integration **+ a strictly-bounded A Small Moment seed** (Option B). Do **not** write the full spec; do **not** make it pure integration (Option A) unless review rejects even the bounded seed; do **not** postpone the seed (Option D); do **not** split A Small Moment into its own lesson.

- **Compact, not full:** like L13/L14/L15, L16 introduces **0 new grammar systems** — it recombines owned engines and adds **one** meta beat. The full template's heavy item tables would be padding; a compact spec captures the recombined target, the A Small Moment seed definition, the deferred set, the scene, the AI guardrail, and the forward hook.
- **Integration + seed, not pure integration:** pure integration (Option A) duplicates L13 and dangles the L19 recurrence. The seed is the safe differentiator **because it is `model-answer-only`.**
- **Not postponed / not split:** L19 *recurs* A Small Moment — L16 must seed it. Splitting it into a standalone lesson over-weights a retention feature and breaks the rhythm.

**Before writing the spec, lock five decisions:**
1. **Seed = `model-answer-only`** reading-comprehension + scaffolded (closed/short) response — **no live AI, no free-text evaluation.**
